NOTES - FLOW DIRECTION GUIDE:
================================================================================
‚Ä¢ External inflows = rainfall, boreholes, rivers, ore water (from nature)
‚Ä¢ Dewatering = underground mine water pumped to surface dams
‚Ä¢ Surface runoff = water from shaft areas, stockpiles flowing to dams
‚Ä¢ Internal transfers = flows within same area (dam ‚Üî plant, etc.)
‚Ä¢ Inter-area transfers = bidirectional flows between areas

POSITIVE vs NEGATIVE VALUES:
‚Ä¢ For all bidirectional flows, the direction shown (FROM ‚Üí TO) is POSITIVE
‚Ä¢ NEGATIVE value reverses the flow to go the opposite way (TO ‚Üí FROM)
